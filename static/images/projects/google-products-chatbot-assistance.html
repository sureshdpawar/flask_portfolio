Gen AI Google Products Assistant

This innovative chatbot, developed using the Generative AI conversational Chat App feature of Google Cloud's Vertex AI.

Use Case: Chat-Based Google Product Search

Overview:

This chatbot application allows users to search for products using natural language queries. The chatbot, leveraging Google's Vertex AI Conversational Chat feature, interprets user queries and retrieves relevant product information from an extensive database, streamlining the online shopping experience.

Scenario:

A user looking to purchase a new smartphone interacts with the chatbot. They type, "I'm looking for the latest smartphones under $500." The chatbot, understanding the query, uses Google's product search capabilities to fetch and display a list of smartphones that match the criteria, complete with prices and specifications.

Technologies Used:

  • Google Vertex AI Conversational Chat: Powers the chatbot's natural language understanding and response generation.
  • Python: For backend development, integrating Google's Vertex AI features.
  • Google Product Search API: To retrieve product data in response to user queries.
  • Flask (Python Web Framework): Manages server-side requests and responses.
  • JavaScript and AJAX: For dynamic content updates on the frontend.
  • HTML/CSS: To structure and style the chatbot's user interface.

Outcome:

The application offers an efficient, user-friendly platform for online shopping, allowing users to find products through conversational queries. It enhances the shopping experience by providing quick, relevant, and personalized product recommendations, leading to increased user engagement and satisfaction.
